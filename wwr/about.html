Our History

Founded by passionate explorers, our rafting company began with one mission: to connect people with nature through unforgettable river experiences. We believe in safety, sustainability, and adventure.

Solomon's White Water Rafting was founded in 1990 by Solomon Alagbaoso, a passionate adventurer and water enthusiast. Solomon grew up near the Imo River in Nigeria, where he developed a love for rafting and exploring the great outdoors. After completing his degree in recreation management, Solomon decided to turn his passion into a business. He started with just one raft and a small team of guides, offering basic rafting trips on the Imo River.
