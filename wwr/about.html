Solo's White Water Rafting Co.

At Solo's White Water Rafting Co., our mission is to deliver thrilling, safe, and unforgettable river adventures. We believe in teamwork, courage, respect for nature, and creating lifelong memories through outdoor exploration.

History

Solomon's White Water Rafting was founded in 2024 by Solomon Alagbaoso Ginikachukwu, a passionate adventurer and water enthusiast. Solomon grew up near the Imo River in Nigeria, where he developed a love for rafting and exploring the great outdoors. After completing his degree in recreation management, Solomon decided to turn his passion into a business. He started with just one raft and a small team of guides, offering basic rafting trips on the Imo River. Growth and Expansion (2024-2025) As the business grew, Solomon expanded his operations to include more rafts, guides, and equipment. He also began offering more advanced courses and trips, including multi-day excursions on the Niger River. Solomon's reputation for safety, expertise, and customer service grew, and the company became a favorite among adventure-seekers in Nigeria.
