          About Us

          Our mission is to provide an unforgettable adventure experience on the water.

          History

          Solomon's White Water Rafting was founded in 2026 by Solomon Alagbaoso, a passionate adventurer and water enthusiast. Solomon grew up near the imo River in Nigeria, where he developed a love for rafting and exploring the great outdoors. After completing his degree in communication technology, Solomon decided to turn his passion into a business. He started with just one raft and a small team of guides, offering basic rafting trips on the Imo River.
